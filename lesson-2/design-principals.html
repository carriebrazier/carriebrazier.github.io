Week 2 Design Principles

White Space and clean design

Sur La Table

Surlatable.com

This is a great use of white space. The design is very clean with very few colors. There are other design principles used as well like visual higherarchy, alignment and contrast.

Rule Of Thirds

Apple

apple.com

The Apple website uses the rule of thirds really well. It also has high contrast with the black and white color scheme. Apple always does a great job with a simple design.

Proximity

Le Creuset

lecreuset.com

There is a lot of proximity on this page. All of the icons at the bottom are similar in distance. There is also the rule of thirds with the color blocks and complimentary coloring as well.
